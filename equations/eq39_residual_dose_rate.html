Equation 39: Residual Radiation Decay

Equation:

\[\dot{D}_t \approx \dot{D}_1 t^{-1.2}\]

Variables:

  • \(\dot{D}_t\): Dose rate at time t [rad/hr]
  • \(\dot{D}_1\): Dose rate at t = 1 hour (unit time reference dose rate) [rad/hr]
  • \(t\): Time after detonation [hours]

Description:

The "7/10" rule describes the decay of residual radiation (fallout) dose rate following a nuclear detonation. This empirical relationship holds reasonably well from about 1 hour to 6 months after the event.
