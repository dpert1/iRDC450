Equation 36: Crater Volume

Equation:

\[V = \frac{1}{2} \pi R_a^2 D_a\]

Variables:

  • \(V\): Apparent crater volume [m3]
  • \(R_a\): Apparent crater radius [m]
  • \(D_a\): Apparent crater depth [m]

Description:

Volume of the apparent crater formed by an underground burst, approximated as half of a paraboloid of revolution with the given radius and depth.
