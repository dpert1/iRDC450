Equation 38: Initial Radiation Dose

Equations:

\[D = D_n + D_{\gamma s} + D_{\gamma f}\]

Variables:

  • \(D\): Total absorbed dose [Gy or rad]
  • \(D_n\): Neutron absorbed dose [Gy or rad]
  • \(D_{\gamma s}\): Secondary gamma absorbed dose [Gy or rad]
  • \(D_{\gamma f}\): Fission fragment gamma absorbed dose [Gy or rad]

Description:

Initial radiation dose from a nuclear detonation consists of neutron, secondary gamma, and fission gamma contributions.
