Equation 18: Radius of Sustained Burn

Equation:

\[R_{SB} \geq \frac{3\sigma_{sb} T^4}{n_b n_t (\overline{\sigma v}) Q_l}\]

Variables:

  • \(R_{SB}\): Minimum radius for sustained burn [cm]
  • \(\sigma_{sb}\): Stefan-Boltzmann constant [J/(K4·cm2·s)]
  • \(T\): Temperature [K]
  • \(n_b\): Bombarding particle density [particles/cm3]
  • \(n_t\): Target particle density [particles/cm3]
  • \((\overline{\sigma v})\): Reactivity [cm3/s]
  • \(Q_l\): Locally available energy per reaction [J]

Description:

Minimum radius requirement for sustained fusion burn, balancing radiative losses against fusion energy deposition.
