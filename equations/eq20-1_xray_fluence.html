Equation 20-1: X-Ray Fluence In A Vacuum

Equation:

\[F(r) = \frac{Y_x}{4\pi r^2}\]

Variables:

  • \(F(r)\): X-ray fluence at distance r in a vacuum [J/m2]
  • \(Y_x\): X-ray energy yield [J]
  • \(r\): Distance from detonation [m]

Description:

X-ray fluence decreases with the inverse square of distance in a vacuum.
