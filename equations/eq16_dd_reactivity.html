Equation 16: D-D Fusion Reactivity

Equation:

\[\langle \sigma v \rangle_{DD} = 2.33 \times 10^{-14} E^{-2/3} e^{-18.76 E^{-1/3}}\]

Variables:

  • \(\langle \sigma v \rangle_{DD}\): D-D reactivity [cm3/s]
  • \(E\): Ion temperature [keV]

Description:

Simplified temperature-dependent reactivity for deuterium-deuterium fusion reactions. This parameterization provides the reaction rate coefficient as a function of plasma temperature ONLY for temperatures <25 keV.
