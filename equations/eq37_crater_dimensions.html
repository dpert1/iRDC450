Equation 37: Additional Crater Dimensions

Equations:

\[R_{al} = 1.25 R_a\]
\[D_{al} = 1.25 D_a\]
\[H_{al} = 0.25 D_a\]
\[R_e = 2.15 R_a\]

Variables:

  • \(R_{al}\): Apparent lip radius [m]
  • \(D_{al}\): Apparent lip depth [m]
  • \(H_{al}\): Apparent lip height [m]
  • \(R_e\): Ejecta radius [m]
  • \(R_a\): Apparent crater radius [m]
  • \(D_a\): Apparent crater depth [m]

Description:

Empirical relationships for additional crater features including the lip dimensions and extent of ejecta, all related to the basic crater dimensions.
