Equation 2-2: Waste Factor

Equations:

\[\frac{W}{P} = \frac{x_p - x_f}{x_f - x_w}\]

Variables:

  • \(W\): Waste (tails) flow rate [kg/time]
  • \(P\): Product flow rate [kg/time]
  • \(x_p\): Product enrichment (mass fraction) [-]
  • \(x_w\): Waste enrichment (mass fraction) [-]
  • \(x_f\): Feed enrichment (mass fraction) [-]

Description:

The waste factor relates the waste and product flow rates in an isotope separation cascade based on their respective enrichment levels.
