Equation 35: Underground Burst Scaling

Equations:

\[DOB_1 = \frac{DOB}{W^{0.3}}\]
\[R_a \approx R_{a1} W^{0.3}\]
\[D_a \approx D_{a1} W^{0.3}\]

Variables:

  • \(DOB\): Depth of burst [m]
  • \(DOB_1\): Scaled depth of burst [m/kt0.3]
  • \(R_a\): Apparent crater radius [m]
  • \(R_{a1}\): Reference crater radius [m]
  • \(D_a\): Apparent crater depth [m]
  • \(D_{a1}\): Reference crater depth [m]
  • \(W\): Weapon yield [kt]

Description:

Scaling laws for underground bursts use W0.3 rather than W1/3 due to different physics governing crater formation in soil and rock.
