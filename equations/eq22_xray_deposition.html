Equation 22: Energy Deposition

Equation:

\[E_{dep}(x) = \sum_{g=1}^{G} \left[P_g F_0 e^{-\mu_g x} \left(\frac{\mu_a}{\rho}\right)_g\right]\]

Variables:

  • \(E_{dep}(x)\): Energy deposited per unit mass at depth x [J/kg]
  • \(G\): Total number of energy groups [-]
  • \(P_g\): Fraction of incident energy in group g [-]
  • \(F_0\): Incident fluence [J/m2]
  • \(\mu_g\): Total attenuation coefficient for group g [m-1]
  • \(x\): Depth [m]
  • \((\mu_a/\rho)_g\): Mass absorption coefficient for group g [m2/kg]

Description:

Multi-group calculation of energy deposited in a material by X-rays as a function of depth, accounting for exponential attenuation in each energy group.
