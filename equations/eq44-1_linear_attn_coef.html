Equation 44-1: Linear Attenuation Coefficient

Equation:

\[\mu = N\sigma\]

Variables:

  • \(\mu\): Linear attenuation coefficient [cm-1]
  • \(N\): Number density [atoms/cm3]
  • \(\sigma\): Microscopic cross section [cm2]

Description:

Relationship between photon linear attentuation coefficient and microscopic cross sections. 1 barn = 10-24 cm2.
