Equation 2-1: Feed Factor

Equation:

\[\frac{F}{P} = \frac{x_p - x_w}{x_f - x_w}\]

Variables:

  • \(F\): Feed flow rate [kg/time]
  • \(P\): Product flow rate [kg/time]
  • \(x_p\): Product enrichment (mass fraction) [-]
  • \(x_w\): Waste enrichment (mass fraction) [-]
  • \(x_f\): Feed enrichment (mass fraction) [-]

Description:

The feed factor relates the feed and product flow rates in an isotope separation cascade based on their respective enrichment levels.
