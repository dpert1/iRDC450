Equation 5: Gaseous Diffusion Seperation Factor

Equation:

\[\alpha = \sqrt{\frac{m_H}{m_L}}\]

Variables:

  • \(\alpha\): Separation factor [-]
  • \(m_H\): Mass of heavy molecule [amu]
  • \(m_L\): Mass of light molecule [amu]

Description:

The gaseous diffusion seperation factor is based on the square root of the mass ratio of molecules being seperated. This only applies to gaseous diffusion.
