Equation 25-2: Time to Max Thermal Power under 15,000 ft

Equation:

\[t_{max} \approx 0.0417 W^{0.44} \quad \text{[s]}\]

Variables:

  • \(t_{max}\): Time to maximum thermal power [s]
  • \(W\): Weapon yield [kt]

Description:

Scaling law for the time to reach maximum thermal power below 15,000 ft.
