Equation 23: Pressure from Energy Deposition

Equation:

\[p(x) = E_{dep}(x) \rho_0\]

Variables:

  • \(p(x)\): Pressure at depth x [Pa]
  • \(E_{dep}(x)\): Energy deposited per unit mass at depth x [J/kg]
  • \(\rho_0\): Material density [kg/m3]

Description:

Pressure generated in a material by X-ray energy deposition. The deposited energy per unit mass, multiplied by density, gives the pressure impulse.
