Equation 13: Plutonium Non-Leakage Factor

Equation:

\[L_{Pu} = 0.43\, m^{-0.15} \ln C + 0.14 \ln m \qquad C = \frac{\rho}{\rho_0}\]

Variables:

  • \(L_{Pu}\): Non-leakage factor for a fissioning plutonium sphere [-]
  • \(m\): Mass of plutonium fuel [kg]
  • \(C\): Compression ratio relative to nominal plutonium density [-]
  • \(\rho\): Compressed density [g/cm3]
  • \(\rho_0\): Nominal plutonium density, 19.85 [g/cm3]

Description:

This equation is an approximation for the non-leakage factor of a fissioning sphere of plutonium, based on data from Bridgman. It is ONLY a valid approximation for plutonium.
