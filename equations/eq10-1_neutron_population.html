Equation 10-1: Neutron Population

Equations:

\[n(t) = n_0 e^{\alpha t}\]

Variables:

  • \(n(t)\): Neutron population at time t [neutrons]
  • \(n_0\): Initial neutron population [neutrons]
  • \(\alpha\): Multiplication rate (bomb alpha) [s-1]
  • \(t\): Time [s]

Description:

Neutron population describes the exponential growth (or decay) of neutrons in a fissioning system over time.
