Equation 15-2: Fusion Particle Number Density

Equations:

\[n_{tar} = n_{bomb} = \frac{n_{tot}}{2}\]

Variables:

  • \(n_{tar}\): Number density of target particles [particles/cm3]
  • \(n_{bomb}\): Number density of bombarding particles [particles/cm3]
  • \(n_{tot}\): Total particle number density [particles/cm3]

Description:

If the bombarding and target nuclei are in equal ratio, such as D-D reactions or balanced D-T reactions, each species has equal number density at half the total number density.
