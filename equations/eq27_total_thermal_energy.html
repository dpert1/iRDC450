Equation 27: Total Thermal Energy

Equation:

\[E_{tot} = fW\]

Variables:

  • \(E_{tot}\): Total thermal energy [kt]
  • \(f\): Thermal fraction of yield [-]
  • \(W\): Weapon yield [kt]

Description:

The total thermal energy radiated is a fraction f of the total weapon yield. The thermal fraction can be looked up in Glasstone Table 7.88.
