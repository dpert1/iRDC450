Equation 15: Fusion Reaction Rate Density

Equations:

\[N_{reac} = (\overline{\sigma v}) n_{tar} n_{bomb}\]

Variables:

  • \(N_{reac}\): Reaction rate density [reactions/(m3Â·s)]
  • \((\overline{\sigma v})\): Reactivity (fusion cross section) [m3/s]
  • \(n_{tar}\): Number density of target particles [particles/m3]
  • \(n_{bomb}\): Number density of bombarding particles [particles/m3]

Description:

Reaction rate density for fusion between two species.

Note: for like-species fusion, such as D-D, there is no longer a distinction between targets and bombarding nuclei. This will increase reaction rate density by a factor of 2, such that:

\[N_{reac} = 2(\overline{\sigma v}) n_{tar} n_{bomb}\]
