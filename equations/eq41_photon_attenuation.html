Equation 41: Exponential Attenuation

Equation:

\[I = I_0 e^{-\mu x}\]

Variables:

  • \(I\): Transmitted intensity [particles/cm2⋅s]
  • \(I_0\): Initial intensity [particles/cm2⋅s]
  • \(\mu\): Linear attenuation coefficient [cm-1]
  • \(x\): Material thickness [cm]

Description:

Beer-Lambert law describing exponential attenuation of radiation passing through matter. Applies to uncollided (narrow beam) radiation.
