Equation 31: Cube Root Scaling (Distance)

Equations:

\[\frac{D}{D_1} = \left(\frac{W}{W_1}\right)^{1/3}\]
\[D = D_1 \times W^{1/3}\]

Variables:

  • \(D\): Distance for yield W [m]
  • \(D_1\): Reference distance for 1 kt yield [m]
  • \(W\): Weapon yield [kt]
  • \(W_1\): Reference yield (1 kt) [kt]

Description:

Cube root scaling law for blast effects. Distances to equal overpressure scale with the cube root of yield, based on energy similarity scaling.
