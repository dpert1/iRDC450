Equation 13: Fusion Yield

Equation:

\[Y = N_{reactions} \times Q\]

Variables:

  • \(Y\): Total energy yield [J]
  • \(N_{reactions}\): Number of fusion reactions [-]
  • \(Q\): Energy per fusion reaction [J]

Description:

The total energy released from fusion is simply the number of reactions multiplied by the energy released per reaction.
