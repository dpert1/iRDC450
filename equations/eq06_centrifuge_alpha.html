Equation 6: Centrifuge Separation Factor

Equation:

\[\alpha = \exp\left(\frac{(m_H - m_L) \omega^2 r^2}{2RT}\right)\]

Variables:

  • \(\alpha\): Separation factor [-]
  • \(m_H\): Mass of heavy isotope [kg/mol]
  • \(m_L\): Mass of light isotope [kg/mol]
  • \(\omega\): Angular velocity [rad/s]
  • \(r\): Radius from axis of rotation [m]
  • \(R\): Universal gas constant [J/(molÂ·K)]
  • \(T\): Temperature [K]

Description:

Separation factor for gas centrifuge enrichment, which depends on the mass difference, centrifuge speed, radius, and temperature. Higher speeds and larger radii increase separation.
