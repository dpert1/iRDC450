Equation 3: Separation Potential V(xi)

Equation:

\[V(x_i) = (2x_i - 1) \ln\left(\frac{x_i}{1 - x_i}\right) \quad \text{[SWU]}\]

Variables:

  • \(V(x_i)\): Value function at enrichment \(x_i\) [SWU]
  • \(x_i\): Enrichment (mass fraction) of stream i [-]

Description:

The value function quantifies the separative work content of material at a given enrichment level. It appears in the SWU calculation and represents the difficulty of achieving a particular enrichment. The separation potential will need to be found for all three streams (feed, product, and waste) when calculating SWU.
