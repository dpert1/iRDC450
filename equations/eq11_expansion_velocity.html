Equation 11: Expansion Velocity

Equation:

\[\frac{Y}{3} = \frac{1}{2} M (u_2)^2\]

Variables:

  • \(Y\): Energy yield [J]
  • \(M\): Mass of fissile material [kg]
  • \(u_2\): Mean expansion velocity [m/s]

Description:

This relation connects the energy yield to the mass and expansion velocity of fissile material in a weapon.
