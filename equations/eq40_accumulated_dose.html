Equation 40: Integrated Residual Dose

Equations:

\[D \approx R_1 \int_{t_a}^{t_b} t^{-1.2} \, dt\]
\[D \approx 5R_1 \left( t_a^{-0.2} - t_b^{-0.2} \right)\]

Variables:

  • \(D\): Integrated dose between times \(t_a\) and \(t_b\) [R or Sv]
  • \(R_1\): Dose rate at t = 1 hour [R/hr or Sv/hr]
  • \(t_a\): Start time [hours]
  • \(t_b\): End time [hours]

Description:

Integration of the residual radiation decay law to determine total accumulated dose between two times. The factor of 5 comes from integrating t-1.2.
