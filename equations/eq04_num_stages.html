Equation 4: Number of Stages in Cascade

Equation:

\[N = \frac{2}{\ln \alpha} \ln\left(\frac{x_p(1 - x_w)}{x_w(1 - x_p)}\right) - 1\]

Variables:

  • \(N\): Number of ideal stages [-]
  • \(\alpha\): Separation factor [-]
  • \(x_p\): Product enrichment (mass fraction) [-]
  • \(x_w\): Waste enrichment (mass fraction) [-]

Description:

This equation calculates the number of ideal separation stages required in a cascade to achieve desired product and waste enrichments given a specific separation factor.
