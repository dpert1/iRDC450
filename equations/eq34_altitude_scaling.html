Equation 34: Altitude Scaling Application

Equations:

\[p = p_1 S_p\]
\[D = D_1 W^{1/3} S_d\]
\[d = d_1 W^{1/3} S_d\]
\[t = t_1 W^{1/3} S_t\]

Variables:

  • \(p\): Overpressure [psi]
  • \(D\): Slant range [ft]
  • \(d\): Ground distance [ft]
  • \(t\): Time [s]
  • \(p_1, D_1, d_1, t_1\): Reference values for 1 kT yield
  • \(W\): Weapon yield [kt]
  • \(S_p, S_d, S_t\): Altitude scaling factors for overpressure, distance, and time.

Description:

Application of altitude scaling factors combined with cube root yield scaling to predict blast parameters at different yields and atmospheric conditions.
