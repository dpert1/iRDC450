Equation 8: Neutron Lifetime

Equations:

\[l_\infty = \frac{\lambda_a}{v} = \frac{1}{\Sigma_a v}\]
\[l = l_\infty L\]

Variables:

  • \(l_\infty\): Infinite medium neutron lifetime [s]
  • \(\lambda_a\): Absorption mean free path [cm]
  • \(v\): Neutron speed [cm/s]
  • \(\Sigma_a\): Macroscopic absorption cross section [cm-1]
  • \(l\): Effective neutron lifetime [s]
  • \(L\): Non-leakage probability [-]

Description:

Infinite neutron lifetime represents the average time a neutron exists from birth (fission) to absorption in an infinite medium. The effective lifetime accounts for leakage losses in finite systems.
