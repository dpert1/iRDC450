Equation 24: Fireball Sizes

Equations:

\[R \approx 90 W^{0.4}\]
\[R \approx 100 W^{0.4}\]
\[R \approx 145 W^{0.4}\]
\[H \approx 180 W^{0.4}\]

Variables:

  • \(R\): Fireball radius [ft]
  • \(H\): Max burst height for local fallout [ft]
  • \(W\): Weapon yield [kt]

Description:

Empirical scaling laws for fireball dimensions at different stages of growth. The coefficients represent different times: radius at thermal minimum, radius at breakaway (air burst), radius at breakaway (surface burst). The fourth equation gives the maximum burst height where local fallout will be produced.
