Equation 26-1: Max Thermal Power above 15,000 ft

Equations:

\[P_{max} = \frac{3.56 W^{0.59}}{[\rho(h)/\rho_0]^{0.45}} \quad \text{[kT/s]}\]

Variables:

  • \(P_{max}\): Maximum thermal power [kT/s]
  • \(W\): Weapon yield [kt]
  • \(\rho(h)\): Air density at height h [g/cm3]
  • \(\rho_0\): Sea level air density [g/cm3]

Description:

Altitude-corrected maximum thermal power accounting for atmospheric density variations over 15,000 ft. Density ratios can be looked up in Glasstone Table 7.85.
