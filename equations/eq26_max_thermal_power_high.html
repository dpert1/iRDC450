Equation 26: Thermal Pulse (Altitude Corrected)

Equations:

\[P_{max} = \frac{3.56 W^{0.59}}{[\rho(h)/\rho_0]^{0.45}} \quad \text{[kT/s]}\]
\[t_{max} = 0.038 W^{0.44} [\rho(h)/\rho_0]^{0.36} \quad \text{[s]}\]

Variables:

  • \(P_{max}\): Maximum thermal power [kT/s]
  • \(t_{max}\): Time to maximum thermal power [s]
  • \(W\): Weapon yield [kt]
  • \(\rho(h)\): Air density at height h [kg/m3]
  • \(\rho_0\): Sea level air density [kg/m3]

Description:

Altitude-corrected thermal pulse characteristics accounting for atmospheric density variations. Lower density at altitude affects both peak power and pulse timing.
